The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) was established in the University on 1st March, 2019 with an aim of continuous improvement of quality for achieving excellence in academic, research and administrative performance of the university. The Prime responsibility of IQAC is to initiate, plan and supervise various activities and programmes that are necessary to improve the quality. It also monitors maintaining quality standards in teaching, learning and evaluation processes. This unit is a post-accreditation quality sustenance measure of the NAAC
